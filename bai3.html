One particular book which is recommended reading isStreet Lawyear by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluating his priorities life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequetly, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless,and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Streets Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and interesting. Other books by John Grisham include The Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The Client.
